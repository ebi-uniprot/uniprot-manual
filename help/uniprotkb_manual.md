Annotations of a UniProtKB entry are structured into the following sections:

- [Function](https://www.uniprot.org/help/function_section)
- [Names & Taxonomy](https://www.uniprot.org/help/names_and_taxonomy_section)
- [Subcellular location](https://www.uniprot.org/help/subcellular_location_section)
- [Disease/Phenotypes and variants](https://www.uniprot.org/help/disease_phenotypes_variants_section)
- [PTM / Processing](https://www.uniprot.org/help/ptm_processing_section)
- [Expression](https://www.uniprot.org/help/expression_section)
- [Interaction](https://www.uniprot.org/help/interaction_section)
- [Structure](https://www.uniprot.org/help/structure_section)
- [Family and Domains](https://www.uniprot.org/help/family_and_domains_section)
- [Sequence(s)](https://www.uniprot.org/help/sequences_section)
- [Cross-references](https://www.uniprot.org/help/cross_references_section)
- [Publications](https://www.uniprot.org/help/publications_section)
- [Entry information](https://www.uniprot.org/help/entry_information_section)
- [Miscellaneous](https://www.uniprot.org/help/miscellaneous_section)
- [Similar Proteins](https://www.uniprot.org/help/similar_proteins_section)

[User manual for the UniProtKB flat file format](http://web.expasy.org/docs/userman.html)
