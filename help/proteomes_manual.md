This subsection of the [Names and taxonomy](http://www.uniprot.org/help/names%5Fand%5Ftaxonomy%5Fsection) section is present for entries that are part of a [proteome](http://www.uniprot.org/proteomes), i.e.Â of a set of proteins thought to be expressed by organisms whose genomes have been completely sequenced.

A UniProt entry can be linked to one or more proteomes. For each proteome, the information displayed in this section consists of a [proteome ID (UPID)](https://www.uniprot.org/help/proteome%5Fid) and a [component name](https://www.uniprot.org/help/proteome%5Fcomponent).
